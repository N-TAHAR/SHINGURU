WORLD WIDE CONTEST

  • Shinguru organizes a international competition for his community every 3 months, with the aim of bringing the community together around the passion of kimonos.
  • To participate in this contest, you must customize and purchase a kimono designed by yourself and check "I participate”.
  • Your kimono appears below and users can vote for their preferred design within the available voting limit. It's time to advertise your kimono on social networks to hopefully win a trip offered by Shinguru to Japan to discover the other side of the story of making your kimono.
